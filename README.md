  - **Eliminates Direct Handling**: You never directly interact with or manage raw tokens, reducing exposure risk.

  - **Full Data Sovereignty**: You retain complete control over whether any usage data is shared.
  - **Privacy by Design**: Your interactions with the application remain private unless you explicitly and knowingly grant consent.

Beyond our core principles, **Friendly Discord Bot** integrates cutting-edge technical safeguards to ensure a resilient and secure operation:

- **üö® Adaptive Rate Limit Detection**: Our sophisticated system doesn't just guess; it intelligently monitors Discord API responses for explicit rate limit headers. This enables:
  - **Dynamic Pacing**: The application dynamically adjusts its request pacing, ensuring smooth, uninterrupted operation.
  - **Proactive Avoidance**: Effectively prevents hitting API limits that could disrupt your **Discord data removal** process.

  - **Exponential Backoff**: Gradually increasing delay between retries.
  - **Randomized Jitter**: Adding a random delay component to prevent synchronized retries across multiple users, a proven technique to avoid overwhelming API endpoints.
- **üîë Token Safety Guidance**: Understanding the hierarchy of security, we emphasize:
  - **OAuth2 as Primary**: Strongly advocating for OAuth2 as the safest authentication method, which abstracts away direct token handling.
  - **Expert-Level Token Mode**: While a direct token mode is available, it is intended strictly for expert users who fully comprehend and accept the associated security implications of managing raw Discord tokens.

  - [Learn about our structured Logging Strategy in our Wiki](https://github.com/TourIT-NL/friendly-discord-bot/wiki/Logging-Strategy)
- **Minimal Permissions**: Adhering to the principle of least privilege, the application is designed to request only the precise Discord API permissions that are absolutely necessary for the specific operations you choose to perform. This means:
  - **Reduced Attack Surface**: Limiting the scope of what the application can access on your behalf.

## üõ†Ô∏è Technical Stack: Powering Your Privacy

**Friendly Discord Bot** is built using a modern, performant, and secure technical stack, chosen to deliver on our core tenets of security, performance, and user empowerment.

### Backend: Rust ü¶Ä - Performance & Safety

At the core of our application lies **Rust**, a language celebrated for its unparalleled memory safety, performance, and concurrency. Rust eliminates entire classes of bugs (like null pointer dereferences and data races) at compile time, providing a highly reliable and secure foundation for sensitive operations like **Discord data removal**. Its efficiency ensures that complex cleanup tasks are processed swiftly.

### Desktop Framework: Tauri ‚ú® - Cross-Platform Power

**Tauri** is a cutting-edge framework that allows us to build truly cross-platform desktop applications using web technologies for the UI, while leveraging Rust for the native backend. Tauri enables:

- **Native Performance**: Access to OS-level functionalities and integration with the system's keychain.
- **Small Bundle Sizes**: Efficient distribution for a lightweight application.
- **Security Focus**: Designed with security in mind, providing a secure bridge between the webview and Rust backend.
- **Cross-Platform Compatibility**: Seamless deployment on Windows, macOS, and Linux from a single codebase.

### Frontend: TypeScript üöÄ - Robust & Scalable UI

Our user interface is crafted with **TypeScript**, building on top of the popular **React** library. TypeScript brings strong typing to JavaScript, enhancing code quality, maintainability, and scalability for our complex GUI. This ensures a stable and intuitive experience for managing your **Discord privacy**.

### Testing: Vitest & CodeQL üß™ - Quality & Security Assurance

- **Vitest**: Our choice for frontend testing, ensuring our React components and utility functions are robust and bug-free.
- **CodeQL**: Integrated into our CI/CD pipeline, CodeQL provides deep semantic security analysis across our codebase, proactively identifying vulnerabilities and maintaining our commitment to **Unyielding Security**.
- **Cargo Audit**: A crucial Rust tool that scans our `Cargo.lock` for known vulnerabilities in our dependencies, ensuring our Rust backend remains secure.

### Troubleshooting Common Issues

Encountering an unexpected behavior? Don't worry, we're here to help you get back on track.

- For a detailed compendium of common issues, their known fixes, and in-depth logging information, please consult our comprehensive [**Troubleshooting Guide**](SUPPORT.md) or explore the relevant sections of our [**Project Wiki**](https://github.com/TourIT-NL/friendly-discord-bot/wiki).
- **Typical User Issues & Resolutions**:
  - **Login Fails**:
    - **Resolution**: Double-check that your OAuth2 redirect configuration is correctly set up in your Discord Developer portal. Also, ensure your system's date and time are accurate and synchronized.
  - **Rate Limit Errors**:
    - **Resolution**: Our application automatically handles most rate limits. If you encounter persistent issues, try utilizing the application's "conservative" rate profile (if available in settings) and ensure the exponential backoff feature is enabled.
  - **Missing Messages in Preview**:
    - **Resolution**: Confirm that the Discord account connected to the application has the necessary permissions to access and read the specific conversations or channels you are trying to scan. Also, verify that the application has been granted the required permissions during the OAuth2 process.
  - **Application Crashes Unexpectedly**:
    - **Resolution**: Retrieve the crash logs from your local system. On Windows, these are typically found at `%APPDATA%/FriendlyDiscordBot/logs`. On macOS, look in `~/Library/Logs/FriendlyDiscordBot`. On Linux, check `~/.config/FriendlyDiscordBot/logs`. Once you have the logs, please open a detailed issue on our GitHub repository and attach the log files for our development team to analyze.
      - [Learn about our structured Logging Strategy in our Wiki](https://github.com/TourIT-NL/friendly-discord-bot/wiki/Logging-Strategy)

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find quick answers to common inquiries about **Friendly Discord Bot** and **Discord data management**.

- **Is this safe for my Discord account?**
  - **Answer**: The application is meticulously designed with paramount security in mind, incorporating features like secure OAuth2 authentication, intelligent rate-limiting, and local-only processing. While no third-party tool can definitively guarantee zero risk due to platform-specific Terms of Service, we strive to minimize it significantly. We strongly recommend using OAuth2 and diligently following our in-app safety checklist.
- **Does the app store or upload my messages?**
  - **Answer**: Absolutely not. A cornerstone of our privacy commitment is that your messages are processed **exclusively on your local machine**. They are never uploaded, stored, or transmitted remotely to our servers or any external service.
- **Will using this tool lead to my Discord account being banned?**
  - **Answer**: We cannot provide an absolute guarantee against account actions by Discord. However, our application is engineered to operate with utmost caution, employing sophisticated API interaction patterns and respecting all known Discord rate limits. Our goal is to drastically minimize any potential risk. We advise users to proceed with discretion and to be aware of Discord's current Terms of Service.
- **Can I recover messages after they have been deleted by the app?**
  - **Answer**: No. Once messages are permanently deleted through **Friendly Discord Bot**, they cannot be recovered by the application. We emphasize the critical importance of backing up any messages or data you wish to retain _before_ initiating any deletion processes.
- **Is Friendly Discord Bot an open-source project?**
  - **Answer**: Yes! We are proudly an open-source project, released under the permissive MIT license. This commitment to open source fosters transparency, encourages community contributions, and allows for independent security audits. Your contributions are highly encouraged!
- **How should I report a security vulnerability?**
  - **Answer**: We take security vulnerabilities very seriously. Please follow our clear and confidential responsible disclosure process outlined in our dedicated `SECURITY.md` file.
